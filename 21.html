What is 21?

21 is a special number in America for a number of reasons.

  1. 21 is one of the legal ages for adulthood in the 50 states.

  2. 2021 was also the year of the modern COVID-19 pandemic in the US and across the world.

  3. 21st California Assembly District is also the home of 21st Century Program as an organization. 21st Century Program is located in the heart of Silicon Valley in San Mateo County.

  4. In the 21st Century, healthcare needs new heroes. We aim to empower the future leaders of mental health to do their best work. UC Berkeley's motto is "fiat lux," or "let there be light." Let the 21st Century be the Century of Light.
